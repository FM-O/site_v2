Ergonomics and design

A website is obviously not only web development. We must also consider two primordial elements: ergonomics and design. Ergonomics, it is primarily allow your users a fluid and intuitive browse on your website. It's also allow him to immediately find the information he's looking for. Have you ever left a website 1 minute after entering frustrated not find what you are looking for easily ? Ergonomics serves this : ensure that your user stay on your website. The design of a website, is probably as important as ergonomics of it. Indeed, a reflection should be made before conception step to combine browse comfort and and attractiveness.

Responsive design

What is a responsive website ? It consists in a website that provides a browse on many devices. Entering the digital age, we have multiplied the ways to go online, multiplied uses. Responsive design is to say "my website is able to adapt to all these devices, all these very different screen sizes". It is very important to think, today, more and more Internet users browse the Internet with touch pads or smartphones. The web has adapted and most serious websites are designed to be responsive. For example, this website is designed for a large screen browse, but also for laptop, touch pad and smartphone browses.
